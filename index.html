BioWeather ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal health data, symptom logs, or location history to our servers. All data processing regarding health correlations happens locally on your device.

To provide accurate weather forecasts, your device's coordinates are sent anonymously to our weather data provider. This location data is not linked to your personal identity or health records.

Your data is used solely to generate personalized health insights and weather risk alerts within the app. It is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

All user data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time by deleting the app.
